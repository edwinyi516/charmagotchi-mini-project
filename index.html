Tamagotchi

Welcome to owning a Tamagotchi pet!
Your digital pet has three critical stats: Hunger, Sleepiness, and Boredom.
If any of the stats hit 10, your pet will die a slow and painful death.
Take care of your pet so it lives a long and prosperous life. Good luck!
*Tips:
  • Feeding your pet reduces hunger by 1, but all the delicious food raises sleepiness by 1.
  • Sleeping reduces sleepiness (go figure) by 1. Other stats are unaffected while unconscious.
  • Playing with your pet reduces boredom by 1, but all the fun increases hunger by 1.
